Let's Explore Airports in Kentucky!

Slideshow showcasing the three airports located within Kentucky, along with a visualized change of Lexington's Bluegrass Airport

Description

Kentucky has three main airports. Bluegrass Airport (LEX), Louisville Muhammad Ali International Airport (SDF), & Cinncinnati International Airport (SDF) They serve as hubs of travel for people to be able to travel faster to places far from Kentucky.

The goal of this project is to show how unique each airport is built and how their structure is differentiated based on how busy and the demand is for people to travel depending on the location.

Visualizations created from lidar data provided by KyFromAbove in ArcGIS Pro and Blender. Additional sources of information from KyFromAbove Phase 2 lidar, 2019, KyFromAbove Phase 2 lidar, 2023, NAIP aerial imagery, 2020, NAIP aerial imagery, 2022, GPS data and photography collected on-site, 2024 name of source, April, 2023.

Page and visualizations created by Maire Birdwell for GEO 409, Department of Geography, University of Kentucky. Spring 2024.
